About us

The Nadhi is a Bengaluru based fashion design firm, privately owned by Ms. Dhiju Anoob & Ms. Nandini Girish.

Chasing a common dream & passion found new hope when Nandini & Dhiju met at National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) Bengaluru. On October 2017, we bootstrapped from our apartments.

Being blessed with daughters we started with kids wear! Every day is a learning for us & least did we expect what was about to happen. Our customers educated us about a key gap in younger generations who wants to style up for an occasion. Our upcoming designs are now purpose made for creative younger generations to layer up in their own style! We ship around the globe & take orders online.

We’ll create style with a greater impact on our society! Everything we choose to do is designed for the best for our planet! There is a social cause too, “A purchase you make, we will help a person in need”
