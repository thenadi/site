About us

The Nadhi is a Bengaluru based fashion design firm, privately owned by Ms. Dhiju Anoob & Ms. Nandini Girish.

Chasing a common dream & passion found new hope when Nandini & Dhiju met at National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT), Bengaluru. Being blessed with daughters we started with kids wear! Our journey throughout fashion college made us aware of the impact textile industry has on the planet. Textile is second largest polluter after oil. We also learned from younger generations about the key issues they face.

Every day is a learning for us & least did we expect what was about to happen. We started leading a conscious living. Being responsible citizens, we choose to create a style with have a greater impact on the planet. On October 2017, we bootstrapped The Nadhi from our apartments.

The Nadhi builds purpose made designs for the younger generations to grow along & layer up in their own unique style. We inspire sustainable slow fashion & less consumption.

Everything we choose to do is designed for the best of our planet. We aspire to be a fully circular brand creating style with great impact on our society. Together we can!
